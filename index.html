Welcome to the Aguamenti web application!

This application has been developed in order to display various data about the quality and quantity of water.

How to use the website?

  • Select the city you want to display the data from by clicking on the map
  • In the pop-up, click on the data you want to display
  • The output for the selected area is displayed as a graph with a three-colors palette for quality of water resource. A green colour signifies good values, an orange one - lower than the European standard for human water consumption, while the red color marks values over it.

In the future it is possible to envision the introduction of an AI-powered search method that will allow, in the case of available climatic datasets, for the real time generation of the predictions completely on-demand.
